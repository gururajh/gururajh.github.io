Led the verification and validation efforts for critical medical telemetry devices and software, ensuring strict adherence to international regulatory standards, including **ISO 13485** and **IEC 62304**.

## Key Highlights
*   **Compliance & Traceability**: Developed comprehensive Test Plans and Protocols that aligned with GDP (Good Documentation Practices).
*   **Integration Testing**: Validated the seamless integration of cloud-synced applications, APIs, and embedded devices.
*   **Standardized Quality Metrics**: Tracked defect density, performed root cause analysis, and maintained clear traceability from requirements down to test execution results.
*   **Cross-Functional Collaboration**: Worked closely with clinical specialists, developers, and product owners to review software requirements and ensure that life-critical functions behaved exactly as intended.
